# AI Assistant Design

## Purpose
The AI assistant is designed to help students use the portal more easily. It gives quick support inside the portal and helps students find the right information or service.

## Why it is needed
Survey feedback showed that students often struggle with:

- confusing navigation
- trouble finding services
- slow or broken pages
- not knowing where to go for help

The AI assistant helps reduce this friction.

## What the AI Assistant Does
The assistant can help students:

- find portal resources
- answer common questions
- explain where to complete tasks
- guide students through simple workflows
- direct students to the right office or service
- help start a support request if needed

## Example Use Cases
Examples include:

- “Where can I see my schedule?”
- “How do I find administrative services?”
- “What should I do if a page is not loading?”
- “Who handles enrollment issues?”
- “How do I submit a support request?”

## Role in the System
The AI assistant sits inside the student portal and supports students during their normal portal use.

Its role is to:

- give quick guidance
- reduce search time
- make the portal easier to use
- send students into a formal request flow when needed

## Relationship to Odoo
The AI assistant does not manage backend workflows itself.

Instead:

- the AI assistant helps students
- Odoo handles structured requests and internal processing

This keeps roles clear.

## Limits
The AI assistant should not:

- make sensitive decisions on its own
- replace staff for complex cases
- override official university rules

If an issue is too complex, it should direct the student to the correct workflow or support team.

## Benefits
Expected benefits include:

- faster support
- easier navigation
- less confusion
- fewer repeated questions for staff
- better access to student services

## Summary
The AI assistant improves the student portal by making it easier to use. It helps students get answers faster and works with the rest of the system instead of replacing it.
