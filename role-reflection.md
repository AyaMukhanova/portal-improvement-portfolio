## My Main Role
My main role on the team was a Software Designer.

I focused on turning student feedback into practical system improvements. My goal was to understand where students faced friction and help design a portal experience that is easier to use.

## My contribution as a Software Designer
My contributions included:

- analyzing survey results to identify common user problems
- turning those problems into design improvements
- simplifying user flows
- improving consistency across pages
- helping define how the AI assistant fits into the portal

## My Contribution as QA & Risk Lead
I also worked as QA & Risk Lead.

My contributions included:

- identifying performance risks
- reviewing reliability issues
- highlighting broken-page and slow-loading problems
- thinking through usability risks

## My Contribution as AI & Portfolio Lead
I also worked as AI & Portfolio Lead.

My contributions included:

- researching how AI could support the portal
- connecting AI features to student needs
- organizing the project artifacts into GitHub

## What I Learned
This project helped me learn how to:

- use survey feedback to guide design
- think about system structure at a high level
- connect user experience with backend workflow support
- place AI into a system in a clear and realistic way

## Final Reflection
This project helped me better understand the role of a Software Designer. I learned that good design is not just about adding features. It is about solving real user problems in a way that is clear, useful, and realistic.
